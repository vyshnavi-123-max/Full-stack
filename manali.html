Explore Manali

From snow-capped mountains to thrilling adventures, Manali is the perfect blend of beauty and excitement.

Famous destinations in Manali

Solang Valley, known for adventure sports; Rohtang Pass, a high-altitude road offering stunning views; Old Manali, for its rustic charm and cafes; Hidimba Temple, a historic wooden temple; Manu Temple, dedicated to the sage Manu; Naggar Castle, offering panoramic views; and Beas River, popular for river rafting.

Best time to visit:

October-February.

Famous adventures you can experience in Manali:

Trekking: Popular treks like Beas Kund Trek and Hampta Pass Trek.
Paragliding: Thrilling paragliding experiences in Solang Valley.
River Rafting: White-water rafting in the Beas River.
Skiing: Skiing in Solang Valley and Rohtang Pass during winter.
Mountain Biking: Cycling through the scenic roads of Manali and surrounding areas.

Local culture

Manali's culture blends Hindu and Buddhist traditions, with festivals like Dussehra and Losar celebrated with music, dance, and rituals. Local cuisine includes dishes like Siddu and Chana Madra, and traditional attire is woolen clothing. The region is also known for its handwoven shawls and handicrafts.
