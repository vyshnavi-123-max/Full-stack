Explore Kerala

Where the backwaters flow like dreams, the hills smell of tea, and the beaches sing in sunset hues!

Famous destinations in Kerala

Alleppey, Munnar, Thekkady, Kochi, Wayanad, Kovalam, Varkala, Kumarakom, Athirappilly Falls, and Bekal Fort.

Best time to visit:

October - March.

Famous adventures you can experience in Kerala:

Houseboat Cruising: Through the serene backwaters of Alleppey and Kumarakom.
Trekking: Chembra Peak, Meesapulimala, and Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary.
Wildlife Safaris: Periyar Tiger Reserve and Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary.
Beach Activities: Parasailing, surfing, and beach volleyball at Varkala and Kovalam.
Ayurvedic Spa & Wellness: Authentic Ayurvedic treatments in resorts and retreats.

Local culture

Kerala’s culture blends Hindu traditions, classical dance forms like Kathakali and Mohiniyattam, colorful festivals like Onam and Vishu, and a rich spice-driven cuisine including appam, puttu, and Kerala-style fish curry. The state is also famous for traditional houseboats and elephant festivals.
