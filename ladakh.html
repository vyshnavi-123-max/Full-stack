Explore Leh Ladakh

Where the mountains whisper adventure, the lakes reflect heaven, and every road leads to an unforgettable journey!

Famous destinations in Ladakh

Pangong Lake,Nubra Valley,Leh,Magnetic Hill,Tso Moriri Lake,Khardung La Pass,Hemis Monastery,Zanskar Valley,Thiksey Monastery, and Lamayuru Monastery.

Best time to visit:

May - September.

Famous adventures you can experience in Leh Ladakh:

Trekking: Markha Valley Trek, Chadar Trek (frozen river), and Stok Kangri Trek.
Motorbiking: Manali-Leh road, Leh to Nubra Valley, and Leh to Pangong Lake.
River Rafting: Zanskar and Indus Rivers.
Mountain Biking: Routes to Nubra Valley, Pangong Lake, and Khardung La.
Camel Safari: Ride Bactrian camels in Nubra Valley.
