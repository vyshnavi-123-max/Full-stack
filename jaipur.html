Explore Jaipur

Where the walls glow in pink, the palaces echo with royalty, and every street corner tells a tale of kings and queens!

Famous destinations in Jaipur

Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, Nahargarh Fort, Jaigarh Fort, Jal Mahal, Albert Hall Museum, Birla Mandir, and Johari Bazaar.

Best time to visit:

October - March.

Famous adventures you can experience in Jaipur:

Hot Air Balloon Rides: Over Amer Fort and the Pink City.
Heritage Walks: Through the old city, bazaars, and historical sites.
Elephant Safari: At Amer Fort and nearby villages.
Shopping: For handicrafts, gemstones, and traditional textiles in Johari and Bapu Bazaar.
Cooking Classes: Learn to make Rajasthani dishes like dal bati churma and gatte ki sabzi.

Local culture

Jaipur is deeply rooted in Rajput heritage — from palaces and forts to folk music and dance. Festivals like Teej and Gangaur showcase its grandeur. The local attire is vibrant, and cuisine is spicy and rich with dishes like laal maas and ghewar. Art forms like block printing and blue pottery flourish here.
